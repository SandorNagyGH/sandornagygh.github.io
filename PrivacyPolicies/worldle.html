Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 30, 2026

Thank you for using the Worldle App (“the App”), developed by Sandor Nagy. Your privacy is important to us, and this Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the App.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not collect, store, transmit, or process any personal information or user data.

Specifically, the App does not:

  • Require user registration
  • Collect personal information (such as name, email address, or phone number)
  • Track your location
  • Collect device identifiers
  • Use analytics or tracking technologies
  • Store gameplay or usage data on external servers

All functionality of the App operates entirely on your device.

2. Third-Party Services

The App does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, or external data collection tools.

3. Data Storage

The App does not store personal data locally or remotely. Any app settings or gameplay states are handled locally on your device and are not transmitted to the developer or any third party.

4. Children’s Privacy

Because the App does not collect any personal information, it does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any updates will be posted within the App or on the page where the App is distributed. The “Last updated” date will reflect the most recent changes.
